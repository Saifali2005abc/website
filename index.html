Introducing the BentoBox Marketing & Commerce Platform. It’s the first in the industry to put diners at the center of how restaurants operate, so you can create better experiences while growing your business.

Know your diners.

Grow your business.

The BentoBox Marketing & Commerce Platform is an all-in-one restaurant commerce engine. With Restaurant Websites, Takeout & Delivery, Events Management, Reservations, and more — all powered by BentoCore at the heart of the platform — it helps restaurants boost profits, stand out from competitors, operate efficiently, and keep diners coming back.

All in one and one-and-done.

Smarter hospitality starts with BentoCore, the engine at the center of the Marketing & Commerce Platform. Here diner data is housed and menu information, marketing tools, and branding materials are all centralized. The more diner-facing modules — like Reservations, and Online Catering – that are added to the platform, the more data BentoCore can collect for a fuller view of what diners and restaurants need for better hospitality.

Meet the modules

The Marketing & Commerce Platform includes a full suite of diner-facing modules that restaurants can activate based on what their diners need. Even better? The modules capture valuable diner data, which restaurants can then use to deliver even better hospitality.
